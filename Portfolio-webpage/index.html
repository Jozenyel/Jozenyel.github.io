My Name is José Ángel Esparza, a Future Full-Stack Web Developer


When I was about to enter college in 2016, I told my father I wanted to study programming. He said that I was going to die from starving if I did that, and obligated encouraged me to go for another career. So I decided to go for chemical engineering


I sucked at chemistry. And I hated it. That's why I decided to try to do something else. After watching some YouTube videos about how to make money online, I decided to tap into the marketing space. Sooner than later, I got hired at a marketing agency


However, the idea of becomming a programmer never left my head. The problem was that I thought that, in order to become a programmer, I had to go to college again. But time was not on my side.


One day of March 2024, after doing some research about AI and marketing, I stopped across an ad of Codecademy, a platform that taught you all you needed to know to become a programmer and get a job. I saw that ad, remembered that I didn't need a degree to get a job in the marketing agency where I was, and thought "This could be my chance". I just needed to learn programming and demonstrate my abilities


I enrolled in Codecademy, selected the Full-Stack Web Developer career path, did all the lessons...


And here we are.
